Dionne Ngo

Film Photographer

Hello! My name is Dionne Ngo and I am 22 years old. I am a film photographer, aspiring to work for models and brands that are looking to capture daily life and free spirits through an organic and vintage lens. Through my work, I found my strengths when I chose to stray away from ordinary ways of photographing views, fashion, and people. I find that a photo tells its story best through candid captures of the simplest things in life.

Throughout the years, I have developed a strong passion for film photography. It was only four years ago I fell in love with vintage photography and the nostalgic feel that film brings to the present moment. While I am thankful for the advancements of technology, I do want to show special appreciation for the old-fashioned ways of creating art.

In such a short time, I have gained so much experience and inspiration through famous flim photographers like Janette Beckman and Jamel Shabazz. Through the help of friends and family, I have found my personal style in film photography to capture each individual's story as naturally as I can. I have photographed for the following magazines: Vogue, GQ, Vanity Fair, and The New Yorker. I have also had the blessing to work with the following actors, actresses, and music artists: Bleachers, The 1975, Bad Suns, Zendaya, Hailey Baldwin, Jhene Aiko, and Cole Sprouse.
